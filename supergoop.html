SUPERGOOP:
REBRANDING

brand experience design

The project consists of a rebranding of Supergoop!, a beauty brand that is focused around incorporating sun protection in all their innovative products. Research was first conducted using STEEPX, blue ocean strategy canvas, analysis of consumer archetypes, surveys, and brand positioning. Using the research, I was able to design potential products and services for the brand that are predicted to be built and made in the near future. The views and opinions expressed on this document are those of Kaylyn Mok's and do not reflect the official policy or position of Supergoop!
