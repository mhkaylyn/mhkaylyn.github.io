World Wildlife Fund:
POSTER DESIGN
FOR A CAUSE

graphic design

The project consists of a design for a potential campaign poster for the World Wildlife Fund. Prior to starting the design, I brainstormed ideas by mind-mapping: picking key words associated with the charity, and writing down any connotations associated with the word. Utilizing the brainstorming, I came up with 100 thumbnails and chose six of them to expand on the idea and design the rough layouts. Then I further expanded three of the ideas as comprehensive layouts. The design I chose was the rhino, which is meant to show that after the poaching of a rhino, the only thing that remains from the rhino is the ivory from their horns.
